Billing Validation v2
"How might we refine billing validation to handle severity sorting, bulk actions, multi-currency, and persona-specific columns?"
Decisions incorporated in this revision:
  • Severity sort: Orders sorted by worst variance first — Over (red) → No rate (amber) → Under (blue) → All match (green). Billing admin works top-down through highest-risk items.
  • Filter row: All (24) | Flagged (8) | No rate (3) — quick toggles to narrow the validation queue. "Flagged" = over + under + no rate.
  • Bulk match: "Apply agreed rates" button per order — one click auto-fills all matching jobs as invoiced at the agreed cost. Only discrepancies require manual invoice entry. Eliminates ~70% of data entry.
  • Multi-currency: Rates are per-currency (MYR, CNY, USD). Display as "RM 450" or "CNY 3,200". Cross-currency comparisons flagged as "Currency mismatch" in amber. Variance calculated within same currency only.
  • Persona-specific columns: Billing sub-table shows Job | Vendor | Service | Route | Agreed Cost | Invoice | Variance | Action (not the ops columns with Rate/Unit/Qty/Status/Proofs).
