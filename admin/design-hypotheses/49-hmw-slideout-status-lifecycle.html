HMW-49: Slide-Out Panel — Status Lifecycle

"How should the slide-out panel adapt to the unified status lifecycle (Pending → In Progress → Completed → Verified) so ops always knows what action to take next?"
Current state: The slide-out shows a proof status chip (Awaiting/Uploaded/Validated) at top, proof upload area in the middle, and a "Validate Proof" button when proof exists. Status and proof are treated as separate concepts.

New model: Single unified status. Proof upload triggers Completed. Verification is admin sign-off. The panel needs to tell ops: "here's the current state, here's what to do next" at every stage. Fees/quantities editable until Verified (billing gate).

Option A: Status Action Bar

Recommended
Thesis: Add a colored status bar at the top of the panel that shows current status + the primary action for that stage. The bar is the "what do I do?" answer. Below it, the panel sections (route, proof, fees, log) are the same — but edit-ability changes per status. Minimal change from current design, maximum clarity.
Pros
• One bar tells you state + action — zero ambiguity
• Minimal redesign — same sections, just add the bar
• Action button moves with status (Start → Upload → Verify → done)
• Color-coded to match the 5-state system from HMW-48
Cons
• Bar takes ~44px of vertical space at top
• "Start Job" button for Pending may feel unnecessary if ops just assigns and expects vendor to start
• Upload is both a section AND a bar action — might be redundant

Decision: Status Action Bar (Option A)

The status action bar sits at the top of the slide-out panel and adapts to each lifecycle stage. It's a single colored bar that answers "what state is this job in?" and "what should I do next?" One component, five states:

What changes per status:
• Pending: Gray bar + [Start Job →] button. Fees/quantities editable. Upload available but no proof yet.
• In Progress: Blue bar + hint "Upload proof to complete →". Upload button prominent in proof section. Fees/quantities editable.
• Completed: Amber bar + [✓ Verify] button (green). Proof docs shown. Upload "Add more" available. Fees/quantities still editable (admin may adjust).
• Verified: Green bar + "Ready for billing" text. Everything read-only. No upload, no edit, no toggles. Fees locked (no ✓/+ toggles, no input fields).
• Rejected: Red bar with rejection reason. Reassign vendor dropdown + button below. Activity log shows rejection event.

Editability rules:
• Fees: toggleable + quantities editable from Pending through Completed. Locked on Verified.
• Quantities (bags/weight/volume): editable from Pending through Completed. Locked on Verified.
• Proof upload: available from Pending through Completed. Hidden on Verified.
• Proof removal (✕): available from Pending through Completed. Hidden on Verified.

Auto-transitions:
• Uploading first proof document while In Progress → auto-transitions to Completed (with toast: "Proof uploaded — job marked as Completed")
• Uploading proof while Pending → auto-transitions to Completed (skips In Progress — vendor already did the work)
• Clicking "Verify" → transitions to Verified, locks everything, toast: "Verified — ready for billing"
• Clicking "Reassign" on rejected job → transitions to Pending with new vendor
