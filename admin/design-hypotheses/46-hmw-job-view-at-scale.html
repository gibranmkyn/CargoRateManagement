HMW-46: Job View at 30-Vendor Scale

"How should the Job view work when there are 30 vendors and 80+ active jobs?"
Scale context: 30 total vendors. On any given day, ~15 have active jobs. ~45 active jobs total: 2 rejected, 9 with proof uploaded, 34 awaiting proof. The ops planner can't hold 30 vendors in their head — they need urgency tiers, not vendor lists.

Key reframe: At 5 vendors, the vendor IS the navigation (you can see all 5). At 30 vendors, the proof status IS the navigation (always 3 urgency tiers: rejected → uploaded → awaiting). Vendor grouping becomes a secondary lens for batching calls.

Step 1: Open Jobs page → "Active" view (default)

Default on page load
Flat table showing all active jobs. The proof pills with count badges are the "where do I start?" answer. Red badge on Rejected draws attention first. The table is sorted by pickup date (soonest first). Filters let you narrow by service or vendor. Group by stays at "None" — the flat table is the power-user default at this scale.

The proof chasing workflow at 30 vendors: Filter to "Awaiting" → toggle "Group by: Vendor". You see 15 vendor headers sorted by most outstanding, all collapsed. Gonda has 5 — expand, review their jobs, call them, collapse. SevenSeas has 4 — expand, call, collapse. Work your way down. The collapsed headers ARE your todo list. The "Vendor" column disappears from the table since it's in the header — more room for route and cost data.

Decision: Proof-first navigation, vendor grouping on demand

Default view: Flat table with "Active" proof pill selected. At 30 vendors, the proof pills (Rejected → Uploaded → Awaiting) are the natural task queue. They scale to any number of vendors because the urgency tiers are always 3 buckets.

Group by: Vendor is the secondary action for proof chasing. When viewing "Awaiting" jobs, toggling Group by: Vendor creates a prioritized vendor checklist with collapsed headers sorted by most outstanding. This captures the user-friendliness of the Vendor Workboard (Option C from HMW-44) without making it the default paradigm.

The daily workflow:
1. Open Jobs → see badge counts: Rejected (2), Uploaded (9), Awaiting (34)
2. Click Rejected → handle 2 fires in the flat view
3. Click Uploaded → validate 9 proofs in the flat view
4. Click Awaiting → toggle Group by: Vendor → chase 15 vendors top-to-bottom
5. For dispatch: click FM service pill + flat view = all trucking jobs sorted by time

Design details:
• Vendor dropdown with search (30 vendors need autocomplete)
• Collapsed groups sorted by most outstanding jobs (not alphabetical)
• Vendor column auto-hides when grouped by vendor (shown in header instead)
• Rejected view swaps Proof column for Reason column (shows rejection reason)
• Click job row → same slide-out panel (proof, fees, activity)
• Click DO link → navigates to Delivery Orders with that order expanded
